I am starting this blog as a place for notes, project writeups, and longer thoughts that do not fit neatly on a profile page.

The plan is to keep it lightweight: write Markdown, commit it to the repository, and let GitHub Pages publish it as a static site.

Future posts will probably cover what I am building, what I am learning, and the technical decisions behind those projects.
